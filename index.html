About

I'm studying Information and Computing Science at UESTC's School of Mathematical Sciences — currently in my second year.

Since April 2025, I've been doing undergrad research at the Future Media Research Center with Prof. Chaoning Zhang, mostly around multimodal model compression and retrieval-augmented generation.

Research Interests

  • Efficient and reliable multimodal large language models
  • Multimodal retrieval-augmented generation
  • Adaptive context and visual-token compression
  • Efficient multimodal reasoning
